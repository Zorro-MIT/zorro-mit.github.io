Browse Safely

Explore the internet without the constant pressence of ads. Browse the internet knowing that your data is safe; Zorro blocks third-party cookies from tracking your online activity. Unlike Adblock, sites using cookies like Forbes don't block Zorro users from accessing their sites.

How do I use Zorro?

Here is how you install Zorro, and how you can start using it today.

Browser Guardian

Download Zorro Chrome Extension

Start by installing the Zorro Chrome Extension. Zorro is available on the Chrome Web Store

  • Install
Browse with Zorro Topbar

interest-based ads

When you browser casually, a Zorro Topbar will show up on certain websites to show you ads curated for your interests. Click on the Topbar to see the ad, or take a look at the price trend for a particular ad.

Browsing Analytics

Examine the Value of Your Data

Go to the Zorro Dashboard to see statistics and analytics on your click-through rate behavior. You can also see detailed browsing analytics, thoroughly examine your curation of ads, and add/remove your interests.
